The Founding of a Classic

It all started with a simple idea: ice cream should be real. No funny business, no hard-to-pronounce ingredients. Just fresh milk, real sugar, and the best local flavors we could find.

We dusted off Grandma's old recipe book and fired up our first small-batch churner with a mission to bring back the joy of the neighborhood ice cream parlor. A place where families and friends could gather, share a laugh, and enjoy a scoop made with love.

Today, Scoop & Swirl Creamery is our dream come true. We're proud to serve our community in Majrakath, one timeless, handmade scoop at a time. Thanks for being part of our story.
